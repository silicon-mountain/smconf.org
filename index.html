Event FAQs

When and where is SMConf 2016 taking place?

SMConf 2016 will be held June 18, 2016 at Chariot Hotel Complex in Mile 18, Buea. The Chariot Hotel Complex is located 1 mile from Mile 17 in Buea and 70.1 km from the Douala International Airport.For information on driving directions and garage parking click here. The conference will include a full day divided between general sessions in the morning and three difference breakout sessions in the afternoon: Design Studios, Hackers Sandbox and Community Pavilion. We will also have a Startup booth after Lunch for Developers and Designers to have one-on-one sessions with Startups.

What is included in my ticket?

Admission to the conference featuring presentations by Designer and Developers, Partners, Sponsors and other great content from the Silicon Mountain community. This includes complimentary admission to the Conference halls, Breakfast, Lunch, After Party and much more.

How do I register?

To register for SMConf 2016, please visit the registration page.

What to expect in Buea?

Buea is a historic town, Capital of German Kamerun, Southern Cameroons and West Cameroon. It contains touristic attractions such as the Mount Fako, the Prime Ministers Lodge(Schloss), Otto Von Bismark's Fountain and more. For more information you should check out LCM Tours.
Buea's weather during the raining season can be unpredictable and can change from block to block! The month of June is characterized by rains and temperatures around 26Â°C.
We recommend packing sweaters and an umbrella for varying weather conditions.

What is the Code of Conduct?

Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the Chariot Hotel Conference premises. We want the event to be an excellent experience for everyone regardless of gender identity and expression, differing abilities, neurodiversity, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, age or religion, or other protected category.

Treat everyone with respect. You are invited to speak up if you see or hear something. You are empowered to politely engage when you or others are disrespected. Also, assume positive intent. If a participant engages in harassing or uncomfortable behavior, the conference organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning or expelling the offender from the conference with no refund. If you are being harassed or feel uncomfortable, notice that someone else is being harassed, or have any other concerns, please contact a member of the conference staff immediately.
